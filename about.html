We make sure the right people hear your story.

Aggrandize PR is a media outreach agency for founders and brands who are done being invisible to the press.

Our approach

Most PR fails because it's generic. We start with research: who covers your category, what angles they've published, and what their audience actually cares about. Then we build a pitch that earns a "yes" instead of a delete.

Research first

Every campaign begins with a media map — the journalists, newsletters, and podcasts whose beat overlaps yours. No list is reused across clients.

Relationships over volume

We'd rather land one feature in a tier-one outlet than fifty in places no one reads. Relevance beats reach.

Radical transparency

You get a live dashboard of every pitch sent, every reply, and every placement live. No black boxes.

Who we work with

  • Early-stage startups raising their Series A–C who need category credibility
  • Consumer and B2B brands launching a new product or pivot
  • Founders building a personal thought-leadership platform
  • Agencies and consultancies that white-label our outreach
