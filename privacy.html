Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Aggrandize PR ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you provide: name, email address, company, and message content when you submit our contact form or email us.
  • Usage data: anonymous analytics about how visitors use this site (pages viewed, approximate location), collected via privacy-respecting analytics.
  • Cookies: minimal cookies required for the site to function and to remember preferences. We do not use advertising trackers.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our services
  • To send you information you've requested (e.g., a proposal)
  • To improve our website and outreach

3. Email communication & cold outreach

If we contact you by email for business development, every message will identify us, include a valid physical mailing address, and provide a clear way to unsubscribe. We honor opt-out requests promptly and never sell your data.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with vetted service providers (e.g., email delivery, form processing) solely to operate our business, under confidentiality obligations.

5. Your rights

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@aggrandizepr.co.

6. Data retention

We keep inquiry data only as long as necessary to serve you and meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymize it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted connections, access controls) to protect your information. No method of transmission is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
